JH Services

What I Offer

I work as a founding designer and design engineer for AI-native products, spatial computing platforms, and robotics companies.

I ship 0→1 products by coding with LLMs, designing agentic workflows, and prototyping in Unity/Blender/web. From prompt engineering to production UX, I handle the full stack of AI-powered product development.

Services include: agent orchestration design, XR interface prototyping, AI coding consultation, MetaMedium explorations, and founding designer engagements via JH Design LLC.
