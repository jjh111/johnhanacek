What I Offer

AI coaching and agentic systems consulting for founders, executives, and professionals who want to actually use this stuff — not just hear about it. I also take on founding designer engagements for teams building AI-native products, spatial computing, and robotics.

The coaching runs on the JH Coaching OS — a custom adaptive system that learns your business, delivers personalized materials, and gives you an AI workspace that keeps working after our sessions end.

AI Coaching & the Coaching OS

Most AI coaching is a slide deck and a Zoom call. This is different. You get a private workspace with an AI coaching agent that knows your business, your goals, and your style — plus live sessions with me where we build real things together.

The system adapts to you. Whether you're a founder who wants to build and automate, or an executive who wants AI integrated into how you work, the Coaching OS meets you where you are and grows with you.

How It Works The arc

  1. Onboard: You get your private workspace and run one command. The AI coaching agent walks you through intake — who you are, what you do, what you want to build.
  2. Build: In live sessions, we build real things from your business using AI. Not exercises — actual tools, content, workflows, and automations you keep.
  3. Learn: Between sessions, you get homework, fresh materials, and async support. The workspace stays alive with new content personalized to your progress.
  4. Own it: By the end, you have a working AI setup, context docs that any AI tool can read, and the skills to keep going. The workspace is yours forever.
