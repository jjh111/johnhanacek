Interactive Installations

Godish (2015)

Using a Leap Motion controller to trigger a video of lightning whenever someone hovers their hand over the controller. Led to really fun emergent uses as different personalities interacted with the simple piece in varied ways.

God Like (2016)

A follow-up version using a Microsoft Kinect to involve a full body pose. Collaboration with fellow student Nathan Danskey.

Influence (2016)

Made for Georgetown Media Fest - an audiovisual meditation on light and sound. Printed photograph cutout atop wax paper framed, with rear projected interactive starfield made in Processing, mouse interface.

Available online rewritten in p5.js as jhana.zone
