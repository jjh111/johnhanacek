JH Art

Visual Art

Multimedia artist working in interactive installation, virtual and augmented reality, digital illustration, 3D scanning and modeling, and animation and rendering.

Background in photography covering editorial, portrait and landscape subjects. Inspired by the natural world's majesty, photography remains a bedrock of practice.

Writer, storyteller and world builder—most recently focusing on Earth Star, a positive vision for the future grounded in the wisdom of tradition and the realities of technology.

Interactive Installations

Godish (2015)

Using a Leap Motion controller to trigger a video of lightning whenever someone hovers their hand over the controller. Led to really fun emergent uses as different personalities interacted with the simple piece in varied ways.

God Like (2016)

A follow-up version using a Microsoft Kinect to involve a full body pose. Collaboration with fellow student Nathan Danskey.

What if the solution to our pollution results in us thriving beyond our wildest imaginations? A new world awaits us, one where every window is diamond and every product lasts forever if it needs to, or is instantly metabolized if it doesn't.
